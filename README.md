# ChargIST

This project is part of the coursework for CMU (Mobile and Ubiquitous Computing) at IST.

A simple but powerful electric vehicle
charging station management app: ChargIST.

This app gives mobile support to electric vehicles (EV) and their owners. ChargIST
should allow users to find EV chargers in their surroundings, view information about
chargers and their capabilities, their availability , and additional information about nearby
services (air/water car station, toilets, food, etc.).
